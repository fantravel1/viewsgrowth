Frequently Asked Questions

How do I audit my YouTube channel?

A YouTube channel audit involves reviewing your channel branding (banner, logo, about), SEO elements (keywords, descriptions, tags), content organization (playlists, featured sections), and engagement metrics (comments, community tab). Use this interactive checklist to systematically review each area and identify improvements.

What should I include in my YouTube channel banner?

Your YouTube banner should include: your channel name or logo, a clear value proposition, upload schedule, and social media handles. The safe zone (visible on all devices) is 1546 x 423 pixels in the center. Use high-contrast colors and ensure text is readable at mobile sizes.
