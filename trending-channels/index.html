What's Driving Growth This Month

  • Shorts dominance: Channels leveraging Shorts continue to see accelerated growth, with the format driving 40%+ of new subscribers for many creators.
  • Long-form quality: Despite Shorts growth, the top channels maintain high-production long-form content that builds deeper audience loyalty.
  • Cross-platform presence: Fastest growers maintain active presence on TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter, driving external traffic to YouTube.
  • Collaboration networks: Growth leaders frequently collaborate with other large creators, cross-pollinating audiences.

Growth Patterns by Category

Gaming Channels

Gaming remains YouTube's largest category by watch time. Fastest-growing gaming channels combine live streaming (building community) with edited highlight content (reaching new audiences). IShowSpeed and Kai Cenat exemplify this hybrid approach.

Education Channels

Educational content continues steady growth, particularly in tech and science niches. Channels like Mark Rober and Veritasium prove that high-production educational content can compete with entertainment for views. Fireship shows that niche tech education can grow rapidly with the right format.

Entertainment Channels

MrBeast-style entertainment dominates absolute growth numbers, but requires significant production investment. More accessible formats include reaction content, challenge videos, and personality-driven vlogs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the fastest growing YouTuber right now?

The fastest growing YouTube channels change monthly based on viral content and trends. As of January 2026, MrBeast leads in absolute subscriber growth, while newer channels like Shorts Factory show higher percentage growth rates. This page is updated monthly with current rankings.

How can I find trending YouTube channels?

You can find trending YouTube channels through: YouTube's Trending page (shows viral videos), Social Blade rankings (tracks subscriber growth), this ViewsGrowth monthly report (analyzes growth patterns), and YouTube's Explore tab (curated rising creators).

What makes YouTube channels grow fast?

Fast-growing channels share these traits: consistent upload schedules, optimized thumbnails and titles, strong Shorts strategy, cross-platform promotion, niche focus with mass appeal, and either viral moments or systematic community building.
