Channel Keywords

Definition

Channel keywords are tags added to your YouTube channel settings that help the algorithm understand your content niche and improve discoverability in search results and recommendations.

Impact on SEO

Channel keywords have minor but helpful impact on SEO:

  • Help YouTube categorize your channel
  • May influence which channels appear as "related"
  • Assist with initial content classification
  • Less impactful than video-level optimization

How to Add Channel Keywords

  1. Open YouTube Studio
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Select "Channel" → "Basic info"
  4. Find "Keywords" field
  5. Add relevant keywords separated by commas
  6. Click Save

Best Practices

  • 5-10 keywords: Focus on core topics, don't overload
  • Include variations: "photography", "photography tutorial", "camera gear"
  • Your brand name: Include channel name and common misspellings
  • Niche terms: Industry-specific terminology
  • Avoid irrelevant terms: Don't keyword stuff unrelated topics

Example Channel Keywords

For a cooking channel:

cooking, recipes, how to cook, easy recipes, meal prep, healthy cooking, kitchen tips, beginner cooking, [channel name]
