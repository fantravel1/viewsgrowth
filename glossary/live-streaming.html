Live Streaming

Definition

Live streaming is real-time video broadcasting on YouTube. Enables Super Chat monetization, live chat engagement, and builds stronger community connections than pre-recorded content.

Live Stream Benefits

  • Direct monetization: Super Chat, Super Stickers, Super Thanks
  • Real-time engagement: Chat interaction builds community
  • Watch time: Long streams accumulate significant hours
  • Algorithm signals: Live content often gets homepage promotion
  • Content repurposing: Streams become VODs (video on demand)

Going Live Requirements

  • Verified YouTube channel
  • No live streaming restrictions (past 90 days)
  • Mobile streaming requires 50+ subscribers
  • Super Chat requires YPP membership

Live Stream Types

  • Webcam: Simple face-to-camera streams
  • Gaming: Screen capture with commentary
  • Events: Concerts, conferences, launches
  • Q&A: Community interaction sessions
  • Watch parties: Premieres with chat

Growing with Live Streams

  • Schedule regular live sessions
  • Promote in Community Tab beforehand
  • Engage constantly with chat
  • Save and optimize VOD after stream
