Video SEO

Definition

Video SEO is the practice of optimizing YouTube videos to rank higher in YouTube and Google search results. Combines metadata optimization with engagement signals.

Video SEO Components

  • Title: Primary keyword, compelling, front-loaded
  • Description: Keyword-rich, detailed, first 150 chars matter
  • Tags: Related keywords (less important now)
  • Thumbnail: Affects CTR, indirectly affects ranking
  • Chapters: Enable Key Moments in search
  • Captions: Help YouTube understand content

Video SEO Checklist

  • ☐ Research keywords before creating video
  • ☐ Include primary keyword in title (first 60 chars)
  • ☐ Write 200+ word description with keywords
  • ☐ Add timestamps/chapters (0:00 format)
  • ☐ Upload custom thumbnail (1280x720)
  • ☐ Add relevant tags (5-15)
  • ☐ Include keyword in video file name before upload
  • ☐ Say keyword in first 30 seconds of video

Ranking Factors

  • Relevance: How well metadata matches search query
  • Watch time: How long people watch from search
  • CTR: How often people click from search results
  • Engagement: Likes, comments, shares
  • Channel authority: Overall channel performance in topic

YouTube vs Google SEO

  • YouTube: Engagement matters more, immediate results
  • Google: Links/authority matter, video carousel opportunity

Many searches show YouTube videos in Google results—optimize for both.

Long-tail Strategy

New channels should target long-tail keywords with lower competition. "Best camera" is impossible—"best camera for cooking videos under $500" is achievable.
