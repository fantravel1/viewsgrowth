Super Chat

Definition

Super Chat is a paid feature allowing viewers to highlight messages in live stream chat. Messages are pinned based on payment amount. Part of YPP monetization alongside memberships.

Requirements

  • Be in YouTube Partner Program
  • Be 18+ years old
  • Live in an eligible country
  • Have live chat enabled on stream

Super Stickers

Animated stickers viewers can purchase as an alternative to text Super Chats. Same visibility benefits at various price points.

Maximizing Super Chat Revenue

  • Acknowledge senders: Read names and respond to messages
  • Create incentives: Goals, reactions, or special content for Super Chats
  • Stream regularly: Build a consistent live audience
  • Engage actively: Interactive streams earn more
  • Quality production: Good audio/video encourages support

Revenue Split

YouTube takes 30%, creators receive 70% of Super Chat revenue. Payments processed through AdSense.
