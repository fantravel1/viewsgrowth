Channel Management

Brand Account

Definition

A Brand Account is a YouTube channel type managed through Google Brand Accounts, allowing multiple users to manage a channel without sharing personal Google credentials. Ideal for businesses, teams, and creators who need collaborative management.

Benefits of Brand Accounts

  • Multiple managers: Add team members with different permission levels
  • Ownership transfer: Easily transfer channel to new owners
  • Separation: Keep business separate from personal Google account
  • Custom name: Channel name independent of personal account name
  • Security: Revoke access without changing passwords

Permission Levels

  • Owner: Full control, can add/remove managers and transfer ownership
  • Manager: Can upload, edit, respond to comments, access Studio
  • Communications Manager: Can respond to comments and messages only

Creating a Brand Account Channel

  1. Go to YouTube settings
  2. Click "Add or manage your channel(s)"
  3. Click "Create a channel"
  4. Choose "Use a custom name" (creates Brand Account)
  5. Enter your channel name

Converting Existing Channel

You can move an existing personal channel to a Brand Account through YouTube settings → Advanced settings → Move channel to a Brand Account.
