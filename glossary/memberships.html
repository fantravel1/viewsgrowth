Channel Memberships

Definition

Channel Memberships allow viewers to pay monthly for perks like custom badges, emojis, and exclusive content. A recurring revenue stream separate from ad revenue.

Membership Requirements

  • YouTube Partner Program member
  • 1,000+ subscribers (30,000 for gaming)
  • Channel not set as "made for kids"
  • No active Community Guidelines strikes

Membership Perks to Offer

  • Custom badges: Appear next to name in comments/chat
  • Custom emojis: Use in live chat and comments
  • Members-only videos: Exclusive content
  • Members-only posts: Community posts for members
  • Members-only live chat: During live streams
  • Early access: See videos before public

Pricing Tiers

Set up to 6 pricing levels ($0.99 to $99.99/month). Higher tiers include all lower-tier perks plus additional benefits. YouTube takes 30% of membership revenue.

Maximizing Memberships

  • Promote during videos and live streams
  • Deliver consistent value to retain members
  • Recognize members in content
  • Offer genuinely exclusive content
