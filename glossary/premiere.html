Premiere

Definition

YouTube Premiere is a feature for scheduling a public video debut with live chat. Creates event excitement, concentrates early engagement, and builds community around launches.

Premiere Benefits

  • Event atmosphere: Creates anticipation and community viewing
  • Live chat: Real-time engagement during debut
  • Concentrated views: Viewers watch together at one time
  • Super Chat: Monetization during premiere (if enabled)
  • Velocity: Strong initial performance signals

How to Set Up

  1. Upload video in YouTube Studio
  2. Select visibility → "Schedule"
  3. Enable "Set as premiere"
  4. Choose date/time
  5. Add countdown theme (optional)
  6. Publish (video goes to scheduled state)

When to Use Premieres

  • Major channel announcements
  • Highly anticipated content
  • Collaborative videos (both audiences tune in)
  • Series finales or special episodes
  • Product launches or reveals

Premiere Tips

  • Promote schedule in Community Tab
  • Be present in chat during premiere
  • Engage with viewers watching live
  • Share premiere link beforehand
  • After premiere, video stays as regular upload
