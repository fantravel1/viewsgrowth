Mid-Roll Ads

Definition

Mid-roll ads are advertisements that play during videos (not at the beginning or end). Available for videos 8+ minutes long. Placement can be automatic or manually set for optimal revenue and viewer experience.

Requirements

  • YouTube Partner Program membership
  • Video length: 8 minutes or longer (previously 10 min)
  • Monetization enabled on video
  • Content must be advertiser-friendly

Automatic vs Manual Placement

  • Automatic: YouTube places ads at "natural breaks"
  • Manual: You set exact timestamps for ad breaks

Manual placement often performs better—place ads at topic transitions, not mid-sentence.

Optimal Placement Strategy

  • Place at natural content breaks (topic changes)
  • Avoid placing during critical moments
  • Space ads 2-3 minutes apart minimum
  • Don't overload—too many ads hurt retention
  • Test and review analytics for impact

Mid-Roll Impact on Revenue

Videos with mid-rolls typically earn 2-3x more than those with only pre-roll ads. Longer videos with good watch time have more ad opportunities.
