Community Tab

Definition

The Community Tab is a YouTube channel feature that allows creators to post polls, images, GIFs, and text updates to engage subscribers between video uploads. It appears as a tab on your channel page.

Unlock Requirement

Community Tab unlocks at 500 subscribers. Once unlocked, posts appear in subscribers' feeds and can reach non-subscribers through recommendations.

Post Types

Polls

Ask questions with multiple choice answers. Great for content ideas, audience research, and boosting engagement.

Images

Share photos, behind-the-scenes content, memes, and visual updates. Can include up to 4 images per post.

Text Posts

Updates, announcements, questions, and conversations. Link to videos or external sites (YPP members).

Video Clips

Share existing videos, Shorts, or highlight specific moments from your content.

Community Tab Strategy

  • Post regularly: 2-4 times per week between uploads
  • Ask for input: Polls about future content increase investment
  • Tease upcoming videos: Build anticipation for uploads
  • Share milestones: Celebrate achievements with your community
  • Repurpose content: Behind-the-scenes, outtakes, screenshots
  • Drive video views: Remind subscribers about older content

Benefits

  • Keep subscribers engaged between uploads
  • Algorithm boost from community interaction
  • Direct feedback and content ideas from audience
  • Lower effort than videos to maintain presence
