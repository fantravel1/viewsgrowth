Content Format

YouTube Shorts

Definition

YouTube Shorts are vertical short-form videos under 60 seconds designed for mobile-first viewing. They appear in a dedicated Shorts shelf and feed, powered by a separate algorithm from long-form content.

YouTube Shorts Specifications

Aspect Ratio
9:16
Resolution
1080x1920
Max Length
60 seconds
Min Length
No minimum

Shorts vs. Long-Form Content

Shorts Advantages

  • Massive reach potential (billions of daily views)
  • Easier to create frequently
  • Great for new audience discovery
  • Lower production requirements
  • Separate algorithm = fresh start

Long-Form Advantages

  • Higher RPM from ads
  • Better for building deep connections
  • More watch time per video
  • Stronger subscriber conversion
  • Evergreen search traffic

The Shorts Algorithm

Shorts have their own recommendation system, separate from long-form. Key factors include:

  • Retention: Do viewers watch the entire Short or swipe away?
  • Re-watches: Does the content loop and get rewatched?
  • Engagement: Likes, comments, shares, and "subscribe from Shorts"
  • Viewer satisfaction: Do viewers watch more Shorts after yours?

Unlike long-form where first 48 hours matter most, Shorts can go viral weeks or months after posting.

Shorts Monetization

YouTube Shorts revenue works differently from long-form ads:

  • Revenue Pool: Ads between Shorts fund a collective pool
  • Allocation: Pool is distributed based on Shorts views
  • Creator Share: 45% (vs. 55% for long-form)
  • Music Impact: Using licensed music may reduce your share

YPP Alternative Path: 10 million Shorts views in 90 days (instead of 4,000 watch hours)

Shorts Strategy for Growth

1. Hook in First Second

Viewers decide instantly whether to keep watching or swipe. Start with:

  • Immediate visual interest or action
  • Text overlay with hook question
  • Promise of value ("Here's how to...")
  • Pattern interrupt (unexpected start)

2. Optimize for Re-Watches

Shorts that loop seamlessly get more views:

  • Create satisfying loops (end connects to beginning)
  • Pack information densely (worth rewatching)
  • Add "Wait for it" moments
  • Keep length tight—no padding

3. Convert to Subscribers

Shorts get views but not always subscribers. Bridge the gap:

  • Verbal CTA: "Subscribe for more [topic]"
  • Create series that reward subscription
  • Pin comment with long-form video link
  • Use Shorts to tease long-form content

Creating Shorts from Long-Form

Repurpose existing content efficiently:

  • Extract the most engaging 30-60 second moments
  • Crop horizontal to vertical (1080x1920)
  • Add captions for sound-off viewing
  • Use best moments as trailers for full videos

Shorts Best Practices

  • Post consistently: 1-3 Shorts per day for maximum growth
  • Add #Shorts: Helps initial classification (less important now)
  • Use trending sounds: Audio trends boost discoverability
  • Captions: Most viewers watch without sound
  • Thumbnail: Shorts auto-select a frame; you can change it
  • Title: Keep it short and keyword-focused
