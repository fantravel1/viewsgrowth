Hashtags

Definition

YouTube hashtags are clickable keywords prefixed with # that categorize content and improve discoverability. They appear above the video title and link to hashtag search pages.

Where to Add Hashtags

  • Title: First 3 hashtags in title appear above it
  • Description: Add anywhere; first 3 show above title
  • Shorts: #Shorts hashtag helps classification

Best Practices

  • Use 3-5 hashtags: More than 15 may be ignored
  • Relevant only: Irrelevant hashtags violate policies
  • Mix broad and specific: #Photography #LandscapePhotography #SunsetPhotos
  • Check hashtag pages: See if quality content uses them
  • Avoid overly competitive: Generic hashtags have billions of videos

Hashtag Impact

Hashtags have minor SEO impact compared to titles, tags, and descriptions. They're most useful for:

  • Categorizing content thematically
  • Shorts discovery (#Shorts, #YouTubeShorts)
  • Trend-related content (#Challenge, #Tutorial)
  • Brand campaigns (#BrandName)

Prohibited Hashtags

  • Misleading or irrelevant hashtags
  • Sexually explicit or vulgar content
  • Harassment or hate speech
  • Spaces in hashtags (use #NoSpaces)
