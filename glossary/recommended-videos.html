Recommended Videos

Definition

Recommended videos are suggestions shown by YouTube in the sidebar, end screens, and homepage. The primary traffic source for most successful channels, driven by the algorithm.

Where Recommendations Appear

  • Suggested sidebar: Next to currently playing video
  • Homepage: Main landing page
  • Up Next: Autoplay queue
  • End screens: Recommendations at video end

How Videos Get Recommended

The algorithm considers:

  • Viewer history: What they've watched and liked
  • Video performance: CTR, retention, engagement
  • Topic relevance: Related to current/recent viewing
  • Session impact: Does your video keep people on platform?

Optimizing for Recommendations

  • High CTR thumbnails
  • Strong watch time and retention
  • Content that leads to more watching
  • Clear topic focus (niche authority)
  • Consistent upload quality
